Robotics & AI Engineer

A machine learning and embedded robotics engineer with expertise in Python, data analysis, and applied AI solutions. I build and optimize intelligent systems with ownership of reliability, calibration, and evidence workflows in real deployments. My work focuses on turning sensing and perception into predictable, reviewable outputs by combining strong fundamentals in robotics and automation with practical engineering discipline.

I build end to end systems across radar and camera based pipelines, computer vision workflows, and scalable backend services. I care about the parts that make solutions production ready: stable ingest, clear data contracts, observability, and failure recovery. I enjoy taking a system from prototype to field operation by improving calibration, performance, and long run stability, while keeping the UX clean and the results easy to verify and trust.

Beyond model accuracy, I focus on engineering rigor-designing systems that handle edge cases, degrade gracefully, and provide clear evidence trails for critical decisions. I've deployed solutions across diverse domains including traffic enforcement, surveillance networks, and autonomous systems, with emphasis on reliability, regulatory compliance, and human-centered verification workflows.
